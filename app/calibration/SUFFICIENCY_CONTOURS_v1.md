## What a contour is

A **contour** names **how institutional certainty forms** under partial observability — not whether a student passed.

```text
constitutional memory of institutional epistemic deformation pathways
under bounded observability and metabolization pressure
```

Contours are **precedent reminders** for facilitators and Atlas deliberation — not heuristics · not classifier seeds.

---

## Epistemic precedence (pathway order)

```text
1. B4-001  →  which surface becomes stabilizable?
2. B8-002  →  how confidence inflates on that surface?
3. B8-003  →  how failure closes before coherence?
```

Surface selection is **epistemically prior** to inflation or closure on whatever was selected.

## Workshop protocol

For each submission under deliberation:

1. **Surface:** which layer did the institution metabolize? (B4-001 lens)  
2. **Success path:** did legibility inflate confidence? (B8-002 lens)  
3. **Failure path:** did illegibility close failure early? (B8-003 lens)  
4. **Band:** assign ecology band + contour band if applicable  
5. **Language:** use registry tiers — no Layer 3 without verdict  

Outputs update Atlas — **not** grader.
